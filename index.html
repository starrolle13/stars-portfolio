My Journey

I was born in Los Angeles, a city of dreams and opportunities. In 2016, I discovered my passion for coding, and since then, it has been an exhilarating journey of learning and growth. Despite the challenges, I pursued my education full-time starting in 2021, balancing my academic pursuits with the joys and responsibilities of motherhood. With the unwavering support of my loving husband and the motivation from my two amazing kids, I've been able to navigate through the complexities of life while staying committed to my goals. Coding isn't just a profession for me; it's a way of life. It empowers me to create, innovate, and contribute positively to the world around me. Each line of code I write is a testament to my passion and determination to make a difference.
